The Estate

40 Acres of Pure
Hill Country Beauty

Magnolia Estate sits on 40 private acres in Dripping Springs — just 35 minutes from downtown Austin — with sweeping hilltop views, a cedar-lined ceremony lawn, and a converted 1920s barn that's become one of Texas's most photographed wedding venues.

We host an intentionally limited number of weddings each year — never more than one per weekend — so your celebration receives the full attention of our team from rehearsal dinner through send-off.
