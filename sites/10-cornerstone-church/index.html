About Us

You're welcome here.

Cornerstone is a diverse, Spirit-filled community of believers committed to knowing Jesus and making Him known. Whether you're exploring faith for the first time or looking for a church home, there's a place for you here.

We gather every Sunday with one purpose: to encounter God together and be sent out changed. Come as you are — seriously.
