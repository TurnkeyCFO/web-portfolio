Restaurant Maison

Chef Lara Voss brings her Paris and Copenhagen training to an entirely local table. Maison is open to hotel guests and the public — reservations are essential and often booked weeks in advance.

The rooftop bar, Alcott Heights, is Austin's worst-kept secret: 360° views, an 80-bottle natural wine list, and zero attitude.
