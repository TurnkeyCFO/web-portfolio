Our Story

Rooted in craft.

Grounds started in 2017 as a single window on South Lamar — a former art space turned corner café that smelled like perfectly-timed pulls and toast. We had one espresso machine, one grinder, and very strong opinions about extraction.

Today we have two locations, our own roastery, and a small-batch wholesale program shipping to 40 Texas cafés. But every morning still starts the same way: someone tasting the first espresso of the day and nodding slowly.

Our Full Story →
Sourcing
Direct-trade relationships with 8 farm partners across Ethiopia, Colombia, Guatemala, Kenya, and Peru. We visit every farm we buy from.
Roasting
In-house on a 12kg Loring Smart Roast. Roasted weekly in small batches. All coffees available within 5 days of roast date.
Training
Every barista completes a 40-hour in-house certification before their first solo shift. We've placed 3 baristas in national competitions.
