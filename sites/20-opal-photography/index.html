About

I photograph light.
People just happen to be in it.

I'm Maya — a photographer based in Austin, TX, shooting editorial, portrait, wedding, and commercial work since 2015. My background is in film photography and documentary work, which is probably why I'm still obsessed with available light and decisive moments.

My work has been published in Texas Monthly, Austin Monthly, Condé Nast Traveler, and featured in campaigns for brands you'd recognize. Mostly, I'm just glad people trust me to show up and make honest pictures.
